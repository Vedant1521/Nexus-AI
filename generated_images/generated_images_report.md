## Why Your Original Cyberpunk Prompt Failed

The combination of the following terms in your prompt likely triggered a **cyber-security/malicious intent flag** in the safety filter:
* **"Cyberpunk"** + **"dark room"** + **"glowing monitors displaying complex code"**

AI safety filters are highly sensitive to prompts that visually resemble depictions of malicious cyber activities, server-room breaches, or cyber-warfare. Adding keywords like "complex code" to dark-room hacker aesthetics is a common false-positive trigger.

### Recommendation
If you want to achieve similar aesthetics in the future, focus on **lighting and composition** while stripping away terms that trigger safety guards (such as "malicious," "hacking," "breach," or "complex code"). Focus instead on "UI wireframes," "colorful charts," "abstract graphics," and "dashboard mockups."
